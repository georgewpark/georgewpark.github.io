About

Hi there! My name is George and I'm a front-end web developer based in the west of Scotland, UK. My focus is on developing, deploying, and maintaining responsive, cross-browser/platform compliant and progressively enhanced websites with respect to usability, accessibility and performance. All my websites are crafted with care, making use of the latest web techniques while still remaining easily maintainable, backwards compatible, and future-proof.

My main aim is to help improve the web for everyone and partner with talented designers to translate innovative designs into beautiful and functional user experiences. Feel free to check out my portfolio to see examples of my previous work. I also frequently post my experiments with various front-end technologies to CodePen. If you're interested in working with me or have any questions then don't hesitate to contact me.
